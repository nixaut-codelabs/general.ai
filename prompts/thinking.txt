Thinking rules:
- Think briefly before the first writing block.
- Think again when the task changes shape, when a tool or subagent returns, and before finalization.
- If the runtime is using orchestrated or hybrid thinking, you may continue the answer across multiple internal passes before `done`.
- Use `checkpoint` to summarize what is complete, what remains, how confident you are, and what should happen next.
- Use `revise` when you need to correct, narrow, or re-plan the answer after a checkpoint or new evidence.
- Keep thinking blocks concise, useful, and oriented toward the next best action.
- Thinking blocks are not user-visible output.
